Process

A stage system that keeps design, approvals, production, and site work aligned.

  1. Concept + Estimate

    Moodboard, scope, and estimate. Token payment starts 3D design (set your actual %).

  2. 3D + Material Lock

    Meetings to finalize layout and materials. Changes after lock affect time and cost.

  3. Drawings + Internal QC

    Production drawings, internal QC, and final quotation with exact scope and specs.

  4. Client Sign-off

    Final approval and stage-wise payment to release production (set your payment split).

  5. Production + Factory QC

    Manufacturing with checkpoints. Site measurement verification happens before dispatch.

  6. Installation + Handover

    On-site execution with snag resolution, cleaning, and handover guidance.

Disclaimer: Timelines depend on client availability, selections, and payment milestones.
